3-Phase Strategy

1 Feb 1–28

Reset & Build Habits

Feb 1 → Feb 28 · Target: lose 3–4 kg
  • Calories: 1,350–1,400 kcal/day
  • Cut sugar, maida, fried food, packaged snacks completely
  • Add 20–30 min walk daily (non-negotiable)
  • Start tracking food roughly (portion awareness)
  • Drink 2.5–3L water daily; start fennel/jeera water morning
  • Eat 3 proper meals — no skipping, no crash
2 Mar 1–31

Accelerate Fat Burn

Mar 1 → Mar 31 · Target: lose 4–5 kg more
  • Calories: 1,300–1,350 kcal/day
  • Increase protein slightly — add sprouts, besan dishes
  • Walk 40–45 min + add bodyweight exercises 3x/week (squats, planks, push-ups)
  • Implement 12–14 hour overnight fast (finish dinner by 8 PM)
  • Monitor weekly weight — same time, same day, morning fasted
3 Apr 1–30

Refine & Lock In Results

Apr 1 → Apr 30 · Target: lose 3–4 kg more = ~10–13 kg total
  • Calories: 1,300 kcal/day (adjust if plateau — take a 1-week diet break at 1,600)
  • Continue strength training — this protects muscle and keeps metabolism up
  • Introduce maintenance weeks: 1 day/week at 1,700 kcal (psychological relief)
  • Focus on body composition, not just the scale — you may gain slight muscle mass
  • Plan your post-April maintenance (1,600–1,700 kcal to keep weight stable)

Eat More Of / Avoid Completely

✅ EAT FREELY (high volume, low calorie)

  • Cabbage — raw, stir-fried, any form
  • Spinach, beans, cucumber, carrot
  • Papaya (great for digestion)
  • Moong dal sprouts (raw or lightly cooked)
  • Ajwain/jeera/fennel water
  • Chia seeds soaked in water
  • Lemon water (no sugar)
  • Besan chilla (filling, low calorie)
  • Watermelon (in moderation, low calorie)
  • Broth-based soups with dal/veggies

❌ AVOID COMPLETELY

  • Sugar, jaggery, honey (even "healthy" ones)
  • Maida — roti, bread, biscuits, noodles
  • Fried food — puri, bhajiya, samosa, chips
  • Cold drinks, juices, packaged drinks
  • Rice (especially dinner)
  • Namkeen, mukhwas, chakli, farsan
  • Ice cream, sweets, chocolates
  • Eating after 8:30 PM
  • Skipping meals (leads to binge)
  • Protein bars / "diet" packaged foods

Key Habits to Not Regain Weight

🥣

Protein First, Always

At every meal, eat your protein source first (eggs, dal, sprouts), then veggies, then chapathi. This naturally reduces carb intake.

💧

Drink Before Eating

Drink 1 full glass of water 15 mins before every meal. It reduces hunger by 15–20% and prevents overeating.

🕗

Finish Dinner by 8 PM

A 12-hour overnight fast (8 PM to 8 AM) naturally reduces total calories and improves insulin sensitivity — key for fat loss.

🚶

Walk After Every Meal

Even 10 mins of walking after lunch/dinner reduces blood sugar spikes by 30% and activates fat burning.

🏋️

Strength Train 3x/Week

Squats, lunges, push-ups, planks at home. Muscle tissue burns calories 24/7 — it's your metabolism insurance policy.

😴

Sleep 7–8 Hours

Poor sleep raises cortisol and ghrelin (hunger hormone). You will crave sugar and eat 300+ extra calories the next day. Sleep is not optional.

⚖️

Weigh Once a Week

Monday mornings, fasted, after toilet. Daily weighing causes anxiety — weekly tracks real trend. Accept ±0.5 kg water weight fluctuation as normal.

🧠

Maintenance After April

After reaching goal, eat at 1,600–1,750 kcal. This is maintenance — not a reward for eating everything. Continue protein focus and weekly walks.

🥔

Carb Notes

Chapathi (1 medium = ~80 kcal): Limit to 2/day max. Don't have at dinner if you're not very hungry.


Potato (~100g boiled = 90 kcal): Allowed in moderate amounts — boiled/roasted only. Not fried. Not every day.


Banana (1 medium = ~90 kcal): Have it at breakfast or mid-morning — NOT at night. Provides potassium and energy for workouts.


Rice: Best avoided during this phase. If eating, only 100g cooked at lunch, never at dinner.


Ghee (1 tsp = ~45 kcal): Don't eliminate — it aids fat-soluble vitamin absorption and keeps you satiated. Limit to 1–1.5 tsp/day total.
